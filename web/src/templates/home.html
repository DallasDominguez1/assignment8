Snail Scale

Accurate Nutrient Information For Those In A Hurry

The Snail Scale Project

Dallas Dominguez Concept Website


Snail Scale is a product for those looking for fast and accurate nutrient information about the foods they eat. People don't want the hassle of downloading a nutrient info tracking app and doing all the tedious arithmetic themselves to calculate their nutrients. We provide all of that information by simply placing simple food on our scale.
